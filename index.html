HOF Philosophy & Scoring
RPL Hall of Fame — Individual Greatness Philosophy
A player earns Hall of Fame induction through individual greatness, sustained excellence, and historical impact — not by winning championships on a stacked team. Championships are a bonus, not a requirement. A player who wins multiple MVPs, earns multiple Finals MVPs, and racks up All-RPL selections belongs in the Hall whether or not they won a ring. Dominance, peak performance, and legacy matter most.

HOF ≥ 55 pts  ·  Ring of Honor ≥ 30 pts  ·  Below 30 = Not Eligible
Peak bonus: +5 pts for a season where a player wins MVP + is on 1st Team All-RPL in the same year (dominant season recognition)
